Selena Dutton

Dancing Through Life

Performer in Branson Missouri

Selena's family were entertainers in one of the Live Music Capital of the World: Branson Missouri! This meant that Selena has been involved in show business since she was born. Her first time on stage was when she was six months old. The Dutton Family specializes in live entertainment, performing between 5-10 shows a week, year round. This opportunity has allowed Selena Dutton to be featured on 5 national TV Shows, 2 radio stations, and to travel across the continental United States Performing in Different Venues.

Mission in Hawaii

After her Freshman year, Selena took a break from school to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. This would be completely volunteer work that would last a year and a half. She didn’t know where God needed her, and later found that the church was going to send her to serve in the Islands of Hawaii! Here she had the opportunity to serve people from the Islands of Kauai, Maui, and Oahu. The people, culture, and land became something very special to her and she served and came closer to God.

There were many economic circumstances that the people of Hawaii had little control over. One of these was the price of living. One thing we did as missionaries was serve in a foodbank in order to help combat food insecurity

Exploring Experience Design

Now back at BYU Selena has shown interest in the Marriott School of Business. Specifically Experience Design and Management. This program focuses on how to design, deliver, and manage impactful experiences! Every Business uses experiences, and Experience design teaches how to manage experiences to increase the bottom line of the company and increase customer retention. Selena’s interest in this major is connected largely to her background. She grew up working where the product was an experience. She also has strong connections to tourist destinations including Hawaii and Branson Missouri. Her love for people, creativity, and problem solving makes ExDM highly compatible for her. Selena hopes to get into the major this summer.
